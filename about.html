Our Story

Founded in 2010, Uwezo Poultry Farm began as a small family operation with just 50 chickens. What started as a passion project quickly grew into a thriving business as word spread about the exceptional quality of our poultry products.

Today, we operate a state-of-the-art facility spanning 50 acres, where we raise over 10,000 chickens using sustainable, ethical farming practices. Our commitment to quality and animal welfare has made us a trusted name in the region.

We take pride in being a family-run business that values tradition while embracing innovation to deliver the best products to our customers.

Our Values

Animal Welfare

We prioritize the health and happiness of our chickens, providing them with spacious living conditions and a natural diet.

Sustainability

Our farming practices minimize environmental impact through renewable energy, waste reduction, and responsible land management.

Quality

From farm to table, we maintain the highest standards to ensure our products are nutritious, delicious, and safe.

Community

We support local businesses, provide jobs in our community, and educate future farmers about sustainable practices.

Innovation

We continuously improve our methods through research and technology to enhance animal welfare and product quality.

Transparency

We welcome visitors to see our operations firsthand and maintain open communication about our farming practices.
