Our Story

Founded in 2010, Uwezo Improved kienyeji Poultry Farm has grown from a small family operation to one of Nairobi's most trusted sources for premium poultry products. Our name "Uwezo" means "capability" in Swahili, reflecting our commitment to excellence in everything we do.

Sustainable

Eco-friendly farming practices that protect the environment

Healthy

No antibiotics or hormones, just natural goodness

Quality

We ensure optimum quality of chicks by timely vaccination and close monitoring of illness

Innovation

We continuously improve our methods through research and technology to enhance animal welfare and product quality.
